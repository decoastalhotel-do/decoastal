✦ Dip & Dine

Your Private Oasis

Our pool area is designed for pure relaxation. Surrounded by loungers, shade, and tropical landscaping, it offers a resort-style escape right in the heart of Effurun.

Take a refreshing dip, stretch out on a sun lounger with a cocktail in hand, or enjoy light bites delivered poolside. The pool is perfect for solo unwinding, family afternoons, or casual hangouts with friends.

Pool access is available to all guests. Weekend pool parties and special events are hosted regularly—follow us on social media for updates.
