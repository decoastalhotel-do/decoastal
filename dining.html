✦ Fine Dining

Warri's Finest Flavours

At De Coastal Place, dining is an experience. Our kitchen blends the rich, bold flavours of Nigerian cuisine with carefully prepared continental dishes—ensuring every meal is a celebration of taste.

From the warmth of fresh pepper soup to the comfort of banga rice, from sizzling grilled platters to crisp salads—our menu is crafted to satisfy every craving.

Our bar is equally well-stocked. Whether you prefer a classic cocktail, a glass of fine wine, or a chilled beer after a long week, you will find it here.
