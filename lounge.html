The Coastal Lounge

✦ Relax & Unwind

Your Escape from the Everyday

The Coastal Lounge is the soul of De Coastal Place. An open-air sanctuary where the evening breeze meets soft lighting, good music, and even better company.

Sink into our lounge seating, order a round of drinks, and let the stresses of the week melt away. Whether you come alone with a book or with a group of friends, the lounge welcomes you.

On weekends, the energy lifts with curated playlists and a vibrant atmosphere that makes the Coastal Lounge the place to be in Effurun.
