✦ Our Story

A Coastal Escape
in the Heart of Effurun

Nestled in the vibrant twin-city of Effurun-Warri, De Coastal Place was built on a single belief — that Delta State deserves a world-class leisure destination that truly feels like home.

Whether you are unwinding after a long week, celebrating a milestone, or looking for a perfect evening out — De Coastal Place is your sanctuary.
