About De Coastal Place

A Coastal Escape in the Heart of Effurun

De Coastal Place was born from a simple vision — to bring the warmth and elegance of coastal living to the heart of Effurun, Warri. Nestled in the vibrant twin city of Effurun-Warri in Delta State, Nigeria, our venue is a sanctuary where the spirit of the Niger Delta meets world-class hospitality.

We are a premier leisure, fine dining, and events destination, designed for oil industry professionals, families, couples, and everyone seeking an elevated escape. Every corner of De Coastal Place reflects our commitment to warmth, quality, and the easy generosity of coastal life.

From our expertly crafted Nigerian and continental cuisine to our three distinct event spaces, premium bar, and tranquil poolside lounge — every detail is curated to make you feel welcomed, valued, and inspired.

Our Mission

To create unforgettable moments where the coast meets good living — every guest, every event, every time.

🌊 Coastal Ambience

Inspired by the beauty and tranquillity of coastal Nigeria, our spaces are designed to soothe, delight, and transport you.

🍲 World-Class Dining

Our chefs blend Nigerian culinary traditions with continental techniques, crafting dishes that celebrate flavour and freshness.

🎉 Extraordinary Events

From intimate birthdays to grand corporate galas, our three venue spaces and dedicated team bring your vision to life.

💪 Warm Hospitality

Every member of our team shares a genuine passion for service — making every guest feel like family from the moment they arrive.

Our Location

Effurun · Warri · Delta State · Nigeria

De Coastal Place is proudly located in Effurun, the gateway to the Warri metropolis in Delta State. Our position in the heart of the Niger Delta places us at the crossroads of business, culture, and natural beauty — serving the vibrant communities of Effurun, Warri, Uvwie, and beyond.

Whether you're an oil industry professional unwinding after a week offshore, a family celebrating a milestone, or a couple seeking a romantic coastal escape — De Coastal Place is your destination.
