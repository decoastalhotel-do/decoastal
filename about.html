Our Story

✦ Who We Are

A Vision for Coastal Living

De Coastal Place was born from a simple idea—that the people of Delta State deserve a leisure destination that rivals the best in the world. We envisioned a space where the warmth of Nigerian hospitality meets the sophistication of a world-class venue.

Nestled in the vibrant twin-city of Effurun-Warri, our venue is surrounded by the energy of Delta State's oil and gas hub while offering a serene escape from the daily grind. Every corner of De Coastal Place has been designed with intention—from the open-air lounge that catches the evening breeze to the dining spaces that bring people together.

We are not just a venue. We are a destination for memories.
