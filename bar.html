Raise a Glass to the Good Life

Our bar is the heart of De Coastal Place—a warm, inviting space where conversations flow as freely as the drinks. Whether you are winding down after work or kicking off a weekend celebration, you will find the perfect pour.

Our bartenders are masters of their craft. From classic cocktails to original creations infused with local flavours, every drink is made with precision and care.

Browse our selection of premium spirits, crisp wines, ice-cold beers, and non-alcoholic specials. There is something for every palate.
